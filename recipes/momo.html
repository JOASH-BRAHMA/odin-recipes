Momo (Steamed Dumplings) Recipe

Ingredients for Dough

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour

  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

  • Water (as needed to make the dough)

Ingredients for Filling

  • 1 cup of cabbage, finely chopped

  • 1/2 cup of onion, finely chopped

  • 1/2 cup of carrots, finely grated

  • 1/2 cup of minced chicken, pork, or tofu (optional)

  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce

  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated

  • 1-2 cloves of garlic, minced

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Make the dough: In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add water, kneading until the dough is smooth and elastic. Cover with a damp cloth and set aside.

  2. Prepare the filling: In a pan, heat a bit of oil and saut√© the ginger and garlic until fragrant. Add the onions, carrots, and cabbage, and cook for 2-3 minutes until they soften.

  3. Form the momos: Roll the dough into small balls and flatten them into thin circles using a rolling pin. Place a teaspoon of filling in the center of each circle.

  4. Steam the momos: Grease a steamer basket and place the momos inside, leaving space between each one. Steam over boiling water for 10-12 minutes.
