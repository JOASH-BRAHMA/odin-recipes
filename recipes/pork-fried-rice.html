Pork Fried Rice Recipe

Pork fried rice is a flavorful and satisfying dish made with rice, cooked pork, vegetables, and seasonings, all stir-fried together in a hot pan or wok. It’s a popular choice in Chinese and other Asian cuisines, offering a savory balance of tender pork, aromatic vegetables, and the comforting texture of rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice (preferably cold, day-old rice works best)

  • 1/2 lb (about 250g) pork (preferably pork tenderloin or pork shoulder), thinly sliced or diced

  • 2 tablespoons of oil (vegetable or sesame oil)

  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped

  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, diced (optional)

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 large eggs (optional)

  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce (adjust to taste)

  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional, but adds great flavor)

  • 1/2 cup frozen peas (or other veggies of your choice)

  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped (for garnish)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Optional: chili flakes or a small amount of ginger for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pork:

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.

    • Add the sliced pork and season with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook until the pork is browned and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.

  2. Scramble the Eggs (optional):

    • In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them until fully cooked. Once done, remove them from the pan and set aside with the pork.

  3. Sauté the Vegetables:

    • In the same pan, add a bit more oil. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper (if using), and garlic. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes until fragrant and softened.

    • Add the frozen peas (or any other vegetables you like) and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  4. Fry the Rice:

    • Add the cold rice to the pan. Break up any clumps of rice and stir well to combine with the vegetables. Let it fry for a few minutes so it gets a bit crispy.

  5. Season the Rice:

    • Add the soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Stir everything together until the rice is evenly coated with the sauce. You can also add a little more soy sauce for extra flavor.

    • Return the cooked pork and scrambled eggs to the pan and mix everything together. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, ensuring everything is well incorporated and heated through.

  6. Garnish and Serve:

    • Once everything is well-mixed and hot, taste the fried rice and adjust seasoning with more soy sauce, salt, or pepper if needed.

    • Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot!
