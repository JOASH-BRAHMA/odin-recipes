Pork Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice (preferably cold, day-old rice works best)

  • 1/2 lb (about 250g) pork (preferably pork tenderloin or pork shoulder), thinly sliced or diced

  • 2 tablespoons of oil (vegetable or sesame oil)

  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped

  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, diced (optional)

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 large eggs (optional)

  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce (adjust to taste)

  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional, but adds great flavor)

  • 1/2 cup frozen peas (or other veggies of your choice)

  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped (for garnish)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Optional: chili flakes or a small amount of ginger for extra flavor
